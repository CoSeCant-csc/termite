Class: TermTopicMatrixObject

TermTopicMatrixObject

An object for storing a term-topic matrix and its associated annotations, including a list of term texts, a list of topic labels, the ordering of terms and topics, the visibilities of terms and topics, the selections of terms and topics, and user-defined highlights.
